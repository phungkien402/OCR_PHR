CONCLUSION:
- The product image (may-do-huyet-ap-bap-tay-d2group-kf-65a.png) is a marketing
  photo with the LCD screen OFF — no actual digit readings are displayed.
  No OCR engine can extract digits that do not exist in the image.
- When actual LCD digits ARE present (synthetic test), both Tesseract and
  EasyOCR achieve perfect 3/3 scores.
- Tesseract with binary threshold >50 + PSM 11 is the fastest (0.23s) and
  most reliable for LCD 7-segment digit extraction.
- The pipeline correctly uses Tesseract for LCD mode and extracts all values.
